Reading philosophy is about engaging with fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, ethics, and the human condition. This is my journey through the great philosophical traditions, one thinker at a time.

By systematically reading the works of history's most influential philosophers, I aim to understand not just what they thought, but how they thought—and how their ideas have shaped our shared intellectual discourse.
