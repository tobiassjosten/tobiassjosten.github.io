Hello, I'm Tobias

I solve problems and I build things.
Often with technology, always systematically.

I started programming at age seven and never looked back. What began as childhood curiosity evolved into a lifelong passion for crafting elegant solutions to complex problems. Over the years, I've worked across the full stack—from low-level systems programming to modern web architectures—always pushing to understand not just how things work, but why they work.

Today, I lead engineering teams in building products that scale. My approach combines pragmatic technical decisions with a deep investment in people development. I believe the best engineering organizations are built on strong fundamentals: clear communication, continuous learning, and a culture where every voice matters. Whether architecting microservices or mentoring junior developers, I'm driven by the challenge of making things better.

Beyond the code, I'm passionate about knowledge sharing through writing, speaking, and open source contributions. I've founded multiple businesses, spoken at conferences across Europe, and maintain an active presence in the developer community. When I'm not at the keyboard, you'll find me training, coaching others in athletic performance, or exploring how systems thinking applies to all aspects of life.
